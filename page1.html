Kemsley Crescent Allotments

Broughton, Milton Keynes

Allotment Association (BKCAA)

The Allotment association is run by volunteer plot-holders who are elected at our Annual General Meeting. It is affiliated to the national allotment body, the South West Counties Allotment Association and has a strong relationship with the parish council, who runs the site.

The association enables us to work together for our mutual benefit and provides liability insurance to plot holders and benefits such as discounts on seeds. There is a communal greenhouse to use and a shed for members containing tools that can be borrowed and returned.

We occasionally organise social events. These have included a Family Big Tea with children’s games, BBQs on site as well as the occasional pub meal and annual Christmas dinner.

The cost of membership is £7.50 per year and runs from mid-May each year (anyone joining within a couple of months of the
renewal period will not be charged for the following 12 months).

If you would like an introduction pack, or would like to join, the simplest way is to drop an email to our
association’s membership email account; bkcaa.membership@gmail.com
